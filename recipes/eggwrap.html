Breakfast Egg Wrap

Get ready for the ultimate breakfast egg wrap! Not only is it scrumptious, but it is also perfect for those on the go. Filled with nutritious ingredients like avocado, peppery arugula, and tomatoes, this egg wrap is sure to give you a boost of energy to start your day right. The thin crepe-like egg wrap makes the wrap light and you can wrap it with anything you like. Best of all, this recipe only takes about 10 minutes to make! If you're looking for something new and exciting to add to your breakfast rotation, this egg wrap is definitely worth trying!.

Ingredients

  • 2 x Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of Flour
  • Vegetables (and meat)
  • Spread/Sauce
  • Cheese

Making your Egg Wrap

  1. In a medium bowl, place the eggs, water, flour, salt, and pepper.
  2. Whisk until well combined, and the flour is completely dissolved.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a nonstick frying pan. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread it out evenly. Cook until the egg mixture is completely set, breaking up any air bubbles that form.
  4. Gently slide a spatula under the edges of the egg mixture to flip. Slide the cooked egg wrap onto a plate to allow it to cool slightly.
